### Why use the MCP Server?

The Model Context Protocol (MCP) allows AI assistants like Claude to interact directly with your business data. With the BoondManager MCP Server, you can:

- **Search** for candidates, companies, or invoices using natural language.
- **Create** time reports, opportunities, or contacts directly from your chat.
- **Update** project milestones or contract details without leaving your AI assistant.
